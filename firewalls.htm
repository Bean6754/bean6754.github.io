iptables2

iptables is a userspace firewall that was created for the Linux networking stack (Netfilter).

`iptables` only applies to IPv4, whereas `ip6tables` will apply to IPv6 and `arptables` will apply to ARP.

In most Linux systems, iptables is insalled to `/usr/sbin/iptables`, but a symbolic link is usually found at `/sbin/iptables`.
