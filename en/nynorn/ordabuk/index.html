How to use this dictionary.

      nm. - masculine substantive
      nf. - feminine substantive
      nn. - neuter substantive
      adj. - adjective
      adv. - adverb
      pron. - pronoun
      vs. - strong verb
      vw. - weak verb
      vpp. - preterite-present verb
      prep. - preposition
      conj. - conjunction
